Our Vision

MILLS- IZOMETAL- YOUR END

Mills recognize us as partners. They look forward to working with us. We look forward to supply your needs.

We commit to developing relationships that promote clear understanding, ethical values, and financial responsibility for our mills. We commit to understanding and anticipating their needs as we continually improve quality and service and develop mutually beneficial long-term relationships.

IZOMETAL is aware that markets are dynamic and changing, and that customers expect world-class products, services and technical support. In order to meet these expectations, we continually improve innovate in order to meet and exceed our customers' demands, requirements and expectations.

IZOMETAL

IZOMETAL is an international steel trading group with headquarters in Istanbul. With more than 10 years of experience in the industry, IZOMETAL gives expert quality services to their customers such as sourcing, product development, quality control, logistics and financing by cooperating with various suppliers worldwide. IZOMETAL currently trades in Europe, Middle East, Far East, Africa and America.

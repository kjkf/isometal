IZOMETAL is an international steel trading group with headquarters in Istanbul. With more than 10 years of experience in the industry, IZOMETAL gives expert quality services to their customers such as sourcing, product development, quality control, logistics and financing by cooperating with various suppliers worldwide. IZOMETAL currently trades in Europe, Middle East, Far East, Africa and America.

In order to have strong position in the global steel market, our experienced staff is fully aware to maintain the same level of services from small-family business to global players.

Our steel trading company has close partnerships with different steel producers; enabling us to offer variety of items with best terms and conditions. The aim of our company is to be “your efficient partner” by achieving total Customer Satisfaction across the various supply functions.
